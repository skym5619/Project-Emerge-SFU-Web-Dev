Hi! My name is Casandra!
I’m an aspiring Graphic, Web, UI/UX Designer and Writer in Richmond, BC

I am a third year student studying Communication at Simon Fraser University.

When I first came to the university, I had no idea what I wanted to do. I came in as a Psychology major. I realized after the Advanced Placement test for Psychology wasn’t for me after studying 4 months for the exam. I then set my eyes on law, but then, I was told by my own law teacher that I was more on the creative side. She said that I had the potential to be a lawyer, but I would be bored out of my mind of how linear and repetitive that job would have been for me. She told me I’d thrive in career where I could create things since I created beautiful works of art for her classes (which she then used as samples for other classes, so I never got my homework back). I was always into creating things because it was a place where I could express myself freely and it stemmed from the pure joy I felt when I first earned the Tawny Star (a badge for creative expression) from cub scouts. I became a designer at heart, but never knew it could take me so far in life. I neglected it.
